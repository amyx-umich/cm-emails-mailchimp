U-M CHEMICAL ENGINEERING IS PROUD TO ANNOUNCE

Sharon C. Glotzer as New Chair

Sharon C. Glotzer
Anthony C. Lembke Department Chair, Stuart W. Churchill Collegiate Professor of Chemical Engineering, John W. Cahn Distinguished University Professor of Engineering.

PHOTO: Laura Rudich

An exceptional educator and soft matter researcher, Sharon C. Glotzer, has taken the helm of the Department of Chemical Engineering at the University of Michigan. Glotzer’s academic stature and impact is reflected in her numerous, noteworthy distinctions. Says Glotzer about the future of the department:

“Most science and engineering disciplines haven’t yet incorporated data science into their curriculum, and this is true for chemical engineering. But companies I speak to are desperate to hire not data scientists to whom they must teach engineering, but rather chemical engineers who know data science. That is a huge opportunity for us because Michigan is a leader in data science, and several groups are already pioneering its use to solve chemical engineering problems. We have a major new institute for data sciences and another for scientific computing, putting us far ahead of our peers. We’re ready to work with our experts to develop what undergraduates, graduate students and employers are asking for – a curriculum that doesn’t yet exist for any chemical engineering department.”
